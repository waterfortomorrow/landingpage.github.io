WATER FOR TOMORROW

ABOUT
Water for Tomorrow is an initiative of The Nature Conservancy team in New York. The mission of Water for Tomorrow is to realize a new era of inclusive and equitable protection and management of water where communities have enough clean water for all, including nature. Our vision is a world where all people, communities, and businesses are transparently connected by data, information and resources, enabling communities to ensure that the water we need is available today, tomorrow and for generations to come.

This initial portfolio of projects is an evolving ecosystem of integrated approaches, infrastructure for systems change, that supports institutions, communities, and businesses engaging in informed constructive dialogue and collective action to resolve interconnected water challenges.

Water Intelligence Platform

A place to learn, investigate, connect, and build around issues related to water. 

Water for Tomorrow Board Game

A semi-cooperative board game about water resource management, conflict resolution, collaboration and sustainable decision making.

Water Leadership Development Program

A program desigend to increase the skills of critical stakeholders to be able to lead and engage in constructive dialogue to advance shared water interests and resolve water issues. 

Act on Water Incubator

A 3-month incubator style program aimed at solving water resource challenges.
